    Broadcast Clips

    During my time at the University of Maryland, I have been focusing on my broadcast work. I have taken classes in video journalism as well as broadcast news writing with the ultimate goal of working at the Capital News Service my senior year. Here are a few videos I've worked on for former CNN videographer Bethany Swain's class in the #SwainDomain.

    Her Body, Her Voice

    On Wednesday, December 1, the Supreme Court heard arguments in Dobbs v. Jackson Women's Health Organization. Protesters took to the streets to make their voices hear. I was able to take my camera downtown to record the event. It was my first protest and my first time shooting in the field. Here are some of the voices that traveled from as far as Washington state and Mississippi. ©Abigail Russ, 2021

    Keep Winter Cold: Polar Plunge for Climate Change

    On Saturday, February 12, the Chesapeake Climate Action Network hosted their 20th anniversary Polar Plunge into the National Harbor. Abigail Russ covered the event and spoke with director Mike Tidwell and participant Laurie Hill. ©Abigail Russ, 2022

    Aviel's Path to the Stage

    Courtney Cohn and I followed Aviel Taub and University of Maryland's Jewish a capella group Kol Sasson as they prepared for the Maryland Hillel Arts Showcase on October 16. We attended practices, dress rehearsals and the performace to get the full experience of a new member's path to the stage. ©Abigail Russ, 2021

    More Than Just A Shot

    On November 7, I got to talk with people at the University of Maryland to ask them why they decided to get the Coronavirus vaccine. ©Abigail Russ, 2021

    Halloween Sweet Treats

    Jason reflects on his Halloween and some of his favorite childhood Halloween memories for #j262b. ©Abigail Russ, 2021

    A Race to the Finish

    Cathryn and Daniel face off on Mario Kart Wii's Maple Tree Highway. Who will make it to the finish line first? For #j262b at the University of Maryland's Merrill College. ©Abigail Russ, 2021
